Our Story

How we met and fell in love

Ryan and Nguyen officially met on January 29, 2025, thanks to Ryan's brother, Joshua.

Now you might be wondering… how did Joshua know Nguyen?

Let's rewind to September 2024, when Nguyen moved to Utah for work at Layton Hospital. That's where she met Josh. They quickly became good friends. That's the end of that story.

A few months later, their first date brought them to Sushi Monster. Nguyen showed up and was immediately impressed to see Ryan already holding a cup of sake — the party had started before she even walked in.

Ryan, on the other hand, was smitten the moment Nguyen walked through the door - elegant and spunky. The sushi was fine… but the conversation? FUN.

From that night on, life filled up quickly with ski dates, study sessions, game nights, trying new restaurants, traveling, and attempting an influencer career (which ended after three TikToks). In the kitchen, they discovered just how different they are. While Ryan carefully follows the recipe, Nguyen confidently cooks with pure vibes and a little chaos.

Somewhere between the laughter, adventures, and everyday moments, it became clear that they simply have so much fun together.

Almost a year later, on January 11, Ryan surprised Nguyen with a proposal at Deer Valley after a ski day with Josh once again playing an important role as the paparazzi.

And now here we are, ready to celebrate the next chapter of our lives, embracing the chaos, the challenges, the achievements, the love, and all the fun that comes with it.

What's the weather like in August?

What is the seed paper included with the invitation?

Our invitations are printed on eco-friendly plantable seed paper embedded with wildflower seeds. After the celebration, you can give it new life!

🌱 How to plant your invitation:

  1. Tear the paper into small pieces.
  2. Place it under a thin layer of soil in a pot or garden.
  3. Water gently and keep the soil moist.
  4. Place in sunlight and watch the flowers grow.
